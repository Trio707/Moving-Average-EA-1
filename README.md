# Moving-Average-EA-1
> **Moving Average Trade Management EA** is an automated MetaTrader 5 Expert Advisor that executes trades based on Simple Moving Average (SMA) crossover signals and provides advanced trade management. It features automatic risk-based position sizing, configurable trading sessions and trading days, fixed Stop Loss and Take Profit placement with a **2:1 Risk-to-Reward Ratio**, automatic break-even protection, staged partial profit-taking (25% at 25%, 50%, and 75% of the target), and adverse movement protection through partial position reduction when price reaches 50% of the stop-loss distance. The EA is designed to automate trade execution while managing risk throughout the life of every trade.
